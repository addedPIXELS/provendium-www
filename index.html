What's Provendium?

Running a Province means keeping track of hundreds of meetings, the officers who attend them, and the members who need to know. Today that work is usually spread across spreadsheets, documents, web forms and a calendar widget on the website.

Provendium brings it into one place. The name is where it comes from: a Provincial compendium.

Provincial compendium

What it does

Everything a Province needs to plan its year.

One system for the diary, the people, the meetings and the work around them. Here is the shape of it. The features page goes through each in detail.

A single Provincial diary

Every Lodge and Chapter meeting in one place, with the business of each occasion, planned and published from the same system.

CraftRoyal Arch

A calendar for your website

An embeddable widget for your Provincial website, in month, list or compact form, with venue maps and directions. It matches your site and updates on its own.

Subscribe in your own calendar

Members can follow the Provincial diary, a Lodge, a Provincial role or their own meetings in Apple, Google or Outlook, and it stays current.

Lodge and Chapter registers

Every unit and its officers, the full establishment shown filled and vacant, kept up to date without spreadsheets.

Brethren and their service

One record for each Brother, with the offices and memberships held over time, and a directory you can search.

Invitations and replies

Members invited to the meetings that concern them. They reply in one click, with no login, and reminders are chased for you.

Attendance and the festive board

Who is attending and who is dining, with dietary needs, seating plans and place cards for the DC team.

The ceremonies team

A shared diary and section for the Director of Ceremonies team, with confirmed attendees and briefing sheets for each occasion.

Summons and notices

The summons for each meeting kept with it, and notices sent to the right people, with a weekly digest and safeguards against over-sending.

Milestones

Centenaries and other anniversaries flagged ahead of time, so none is missed.

All features

On your website

A calendar that lives on your Province's site.

Publish once and the meetings appear on your website in a widget that matches it. Choose a month grid, a list or a compact view, restrict it to the calendars you want, and lock it to your own domains.

  • Month, list or compact, as you prefer
  • Venue address, map and directions on each entry
  • Updates itself when the diary changes

The festive board

Attendance, dining and seating, handled.

Members say whether they are attending and whether they are dining, in the same one-click reply. The DC team sees the numbers, the dietary needs and a seating plan they can arrange, print and turn into place cards.

  • Attendance and dining as two separate answers
  • Dietary requirements gathered with the reply
  • Seating plan, place cards and a dietary list to print

Privacy at the foundation

Built for the discretion Freemasonry expects.

Each Province is kept separate from every other. Sensitive contact details are encrypted, and members see only what they should. Privacy is designed in from the start, not added on.

How we protect your data

Who it's for

  • The Provincial Office: the diary, the registers and the administration in one place.
  • Secretaries and Scribes: meetings, contacts and notices without the paperwork.
  • The DC team: who is attending each occasion, and the seating and dining that follow.
  • Members: who want to know what's on and where, and to follow it in their own calendar.
