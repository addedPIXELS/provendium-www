The Provincial Compendium

The diary and administration for your Province, in one place.

Provendium is a secure calendar and administration platform for the Provinces of English Freemasonry. It puts the Provincial diary, the registers and the work around them into a single system, instead of spreadsheets, documents and separate calendar tools.

Get in touch See what it does

What's Provendium?

Running a Province means keeping track of hundreds of meetings, the officers who attend them, and the members who need to know. Today that work is usually spread across spreadsheets, documents, web forms and a calendar widget on the website.

Provendium brings it into one place. The name is where it comes from: a Provincial compendium.

Provincial compendium

What it does

What a Province needs to plan its year and keep its members informed.

□

A single Provincial diary

Every Lodge and Chapter meeting in one place, planned and published from the same system.

◇

A calendar for your website

An embeddable widget for your Provincial website. It matches your site and updates on its own.

▸

Officers and units

Lodge and Chapter registers, and the officers who serve in them, kept up to date without spreadsheets.

✉

Invitations and replies

Members invited to the meetings that concern them, with notices and replies handled in the system.

◆

The ceremonies team

A shared view for the Director of Ceremonies team to plan who attends each occasion.

☀

Milestones

Centenaries and other anniversaries flagged ahead of time, so none is missed.

Who it's for

  • The Provincial Office: the diary, the registers and the administration in one place.
  • Secretaries and Scribes: meetings, contacts and notices without the paperwork.
  • The DC team: a shared view of who is attending which occasion.
  • Members: who want to know what's on, and where.
